Privacy policy

The current demo does not send data to us.

Effective 6 July 2026 · Developer: Javonn Liner · javonn19@gmail.com

01

No cloud account service

The current internal build does not send profiles, messages, events, reports, or media to a Delta Pi Hub server.

02

No tracking or advertising

The app contains no advertising, cross-app tracking, analytics, cloud AI, or remote push-token registration.

03

Local photos and reminders

Test photos are copied into app storage. Event reminders are scheduled locally after notification permission is granted.

Information stored locally

Sample activity and your test media

  • The selected sample profile and local session state
  • Demo announcements, events, chats, reports, and preferences
  • Photos you choose for local media or a sample profile
  • Locally scheduled event-reminder information

Information not collected

No developer-side tracking or profiling

  • No advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking
  • No analytics, crash-reporting, or diagnostic service
  • No cloud AI prompts, account database, or media upload
  • No remote notification token registration

Retention and deletion

You control the current local copy

Signing out does not erase local sample content. Use Reset Local Demo Data inside the app to remove the reconstructed state and copied test media, or remove the app to delete its local container. Android system backup may retain a device backup when backup is enabled in your Google account.

Device and external services

System features act only when you choose them

iOS or Android handles notification permission and local reminders. If you open Maps, an external website, or the system share sheet, the service you select processes that interaction under its own privacy terms.

Audience and children

For adult internal testers

The current demo is intended for adult chapter testers. It is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect children's personal information.

Security and policy changes

Protected by your device

Local demo data is protected by your device and operating-system controls. We will revise this policy before enabling any server service and will post a new effective date on this page.

Production services

Production accounts, cloud sharing, remote notifications, and server-backed moderation are not enabled in the current internal builds. This policy will be updated before any of those services are released.

Support

Need help with Delta Pi Hub?

Contact the Delta Pi Chapter through its official support page. Include your device model, operating-system version, and the screen where the issue occurred.
