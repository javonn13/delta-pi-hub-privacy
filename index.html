Privacy that matches the app today.

This page describes Delta Pi Hub version 0.1.3, currently prepared for internal testing with local sample data.

Effective July 6, 2026

Privacy policy

Your current data stays on your device.

01

No cloud account service

The current internal build does not send profiles, messages, events, reports, or media to a Delta Pi Hub server.

02

No tracking or advertising

The app contains no advertising, cross-app tracking, analytics, cloud AI, or remote push-token registration.

03

Local photos and reminders

Test photos are copied into app storage. Event reminders are scheduled locally after notification permission is granted.

Information stored locally

Sample activity and your test media

  • The selected sample profile and local session state
  • Demo announcements, events, chats, reports, and preferences
  • Photos you choose for local media or a sample profile
  • Locally scheduled event-reminder information

Information not collected

No developer-side tracking or profiling

  • No advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking
  • No analytics, crash-reporting, or diagnostic service
  • No cloud AI prompts, account database, or media upload
  • No remote notification token registration

Retention and deletion

You control the current local copy

Signing out does not erase local sample content. Use Reset Local Demo Data inside the app to remove the reconstructed state and copied test media, or remove the app to delete its local container.

Apple and external services

System features act only when you choose them

iOS handles notification permission and local reminders. If you open Maps, an external website, or the system share sheet, the service you select processes that interaction under its own privacy terms.

Production services

Production accounts, cloud sharing, remote notifications, and server-backed moderation are not enabled in version 0.1.3. This policy will be updated before any of those services are released.

Support

Need help with Delta Pi Hub?

Contact the Delta Pi Chapter through its official support page. Include your device model, iOS version, and the screen where the issue occurred.
