Privacy policy

The current app does not send data to us.

Effective 7 July 2026 · Developer: Javonn Liner · javonn19@gmail.com

01

No cloud account service

The current app does not send profiles, messages, events, reports, or media to a Delta Pi Hub server.

02

No tracking or advertising

The app contains no advertising, cross-app tracking, analytics, cloud AI, or remote push-token registration.

03

Local photos and reminders

Selected photos are copied into app storage. Event reminders are scheduled locally after notification permission is granted.

Information stored locally

Sample activity and your selected media

  • The selected sample profile and local session state
  • On-device announcements, events, chats, reports, and preferences
  • Photos you choose for local media or a sample profile
  • Locally scheduled event-reminder information

Information not collected

No developer-side tracking or profiling

  • No advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking
  • No analytics, crash-reporting, or diagnostic service
  • No cloud AI prompts, account database, or media upload
  • No remote notification token registration

Retention and deletion

You control the current local copy

Switching roles does not erase local sample content. Use Reset Workspace Data inside the app to remove workspace changes and copied media, or remove the app to delete its local container. A device backup may retain app data when backup is enabled.

Device and external services

System features act only when you choose them

iOS or Android handles notification permission and local reminders. If you open Maps, an external website, or the system share sheet, the service you select processes that interaction under its own privacy terms.

Audience and children

For adult users

The current app is intended for adults exploring a fictional chapter workspace. It is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect children's personal information.

Security and policy changes

Protected by your device

Local workspace data is protected by your device and operating-system controls. We will revise this policy before enabling any server service and will post a new effective date on this page.

Production services

Production accounts, cloud sharing, remote notifications, and server-backed moderation are not enabled in the current app. This policy will be updated before any of those services are released.

Contact the Delta Pi Chapter through its official support page. Include your device model, operating-system version, and the screen where the issue occurred.
